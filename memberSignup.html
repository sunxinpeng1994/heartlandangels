Distributions and Carried Interest:

“Member Investors” are Heartland Members who invest in a specific Heartland Portfolio Company via a dedicated Heartland Investment Vehicle created for the purpose of investing in that Heartland Portfolio Company. If a Heartland Portfolio Company undergoes a liquidity event (“Portfolio Company Exit”), cash flows from that Portfolio Company Exit to the dedicated Heartland Investment Vehicle will be distributed upon the closing of that Heartland Investment Vehicle, according to the following sequence:

(i) First, all fees related to the distribution and closing of the Heartland Investment Vehicle will be paid out of cash flows from the Portfolio Company Exit;

(ii) Second, thereafter, cash flows from the Portfolio Company Exit, in excess of payments made in step (i), will be distributed to Member Investors until cumulative distributions equal Member Investor’s capital contributions to that specific Heartland Portfolio Company;

(iii) Third, thereafter, cash flows from the Portfolio Company Exit, in excess of payments and distributions made in steps (i) and (ii), if any, will be distributed to Member Investors until cumulative distributions equal a Preferred Return of 5% IRR;

(iv) Fourth, thereafter, cash flows from the Portfolio Company Exit, in excess of distributions made in steps (i), (ii), and (iii), if any, are distributed 93% to Member Investors and 7% to Heartland Angels, Inc.
