JOIN HEARTLAND ANGELS


Heartland Angels accepts investors with a range of investing experience. Part of Heartland's mission is education - we have programs designed to teach new members about angel investing and provide mentoring on how to evaluate companies. You can be as involved as you want in the company evaluation process, and there is no minimum investment requirement to maintain your membership status.

You can join Heartland as either a Full Member (an Accredited Investor) or an Associate Member (a Non-Accredited Investor). In order to be a full member, an individual or organization must meet the Securities and Exchange Commission definition of an accredited investor.
